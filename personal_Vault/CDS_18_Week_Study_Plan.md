# CDS Exam 18-Week Study Plan
**Target Exam Date:** September 6, 2026
**Time Remaining:** 18 Weeks (from May 4, 2026)

## Weeks 1-4 (May 4 - May 31)
- [ ] **Maths:** Geometry (Lines, Angles, Triangles, Circles) and Mensuration (Area, Perimeter, Volume).
- [ ] **Science:** 9th & 10th NCERT Physics (Motion, Force, Work) & Chemistry (Chemical Changes, Elements).
- [ ] **English:** Parts of Speech (Nouns, Pronouns, Verbs) and basic sentence structure.
- [ ] **GS:** Ancient History (R S Sharma) and Class 11th Geography Part 1 (Geological Time Scale, Volcanoes).

## Weeks 5-8 (June 1 - June 28)
- [ ] **Maths:** Trigonometry (Ratios, Identities, Heights & Distances) and Statistics.
- [ ] **Science:** Biology fundamentals (Cell division, Diversity, Plant/Human Systems).
- [ ] **English:** Tenses, Subject-Verb Agreement, Active and Passive Voice.
- [ ] **GS:** Medieval History (Satish Chandra) and Class 12th Macroeconomics (National Income, Fiscal Policy).

## Weeks 9-12 (June 29 - July 26)
- [ ] **Maths:** Arithmetic (Number Systems, Percentages, Time/Speed) and Algebra (Basic operations, Factors).
- [ ] **Science:** 11th NCERT Physics (Thermodynamics, Optics) & Chemistry (Atomic Structure).
- [ ] **English:** Direct/Indirect Speech, Conditional Sentences, and Articles.
- [ ] **GS:** Modern History (Spectrum - Arrival of Europeans to 1857 Revolt) and Class 11th Geography Part 2.

## Weeks 13-15 (July 27 - August 16)
- [ ] **Maths:** HCF/LCM, Polynomials, and Quadratic Equations.
- [ ] **Science:** Review all Biology and 10th NCERT topics.
- [ ] **English:** Phrasal Verbs, Question Tags, and extensive reading practice.
- [ ] **GS:** Complete Modern History (Gandhian Era, Post-Independence) and Economics (Monetary Policy, NITI Aayog).

## Weeks 16-17 (August 17 - August 30)
- [ ] **Maths:** Mock tests and advanced problem-solving for Geometry and Trigonometry.
- [ ] **Current Affairs:** Defence, Sports, Sci-Tech, Indexes, and International Summits (Jan-Aug 2026).
- [ ] **English:** Practice reading comprehensions and previous year papers.
- [ ] **GS:** Revision of History, Economics, and Geography highlights.

## Week 18 (August 31 - September 5)
- [ ] **Revision:** Skim all formulas, vocabulary lists, and critical historical timelines.
- [ ] **Practice:** Only attempt light mock questions; avoid starting new topics.
- [ ] **Rest:** Ensure adequate sleep and organize exam day logistics.
