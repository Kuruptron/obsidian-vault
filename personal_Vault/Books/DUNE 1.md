## 📝 Quick Summary
> [!ABSTRACT] One-Sentence Pitch
> A young noble becomes the messiah of a desert planet while fighting for control of the galaxy's most valuable substance: spice.

## 🗝️ Key Takeaways
* **Politics and Ecology:** How environment shapes culture and power structures.
* **The Danger of Messiahs:** Herbert’s warning against following charismatic leaders blindly.

## ✍️ My Notes
- The world-building is incredibly dense; need to keep track of the terminology (Bene Gesserit, Kwisatz Haderach).
- The internal monologues provide a lot of depth to Paul's internal conflict.
